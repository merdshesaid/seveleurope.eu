# 🍦 Sevel Europe

> A modern, high-performance business website built for **Sevel Europe**, showcasing commercial refrigeration and food display equipment.

🌐 **Live Website:** https://seveleurope.eu

---

# Overview

Sevel Europe is a professionally developed website designed to present a large catalog of commercial refrigeration products while maintaining excellent performance, responsiveness, and user experience.

The project focuses on creating an intuitive browsing experience for customers looking for professional equipment such as:

- 🍦 Ice Cream Display Showcases
- 🥐 Pastry Display Cabinets
- 🍰 Cake Display Refrigerators
- 🥩 Meat Display Counters
- 🧀 Deli Refrigeration Units
- 🥤 Beverage Coolers
- ❄️ Commercial Refrigeration Solutions
- 🏪 Retail Display Equipment

Unlike a simple landing page, this project required building a scalable structure capable of presenting numerous product categories while remaining easy to navigate across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

# Technical Challenges

Although the website appears clean and simple from the user's perspective, significant work went into designing a scalable architecture capable of supporting a growing catalog of commercial products.

Some of the challenges included:

- Organizing multiple product categories
- Maintaining a consistent design across the entire catalog
- Optimizing loading performance despite numerous product pages
- Creating responsive layouts for all screen sizes
- Ensuring maintainability for future product additions
- Deploying the project with production-ready hosting

The goal was to make browsing hundreds of commercial refrigeration products feel effortless.
